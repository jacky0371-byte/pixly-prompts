# 服装类型修饰器 — 上装

**版本：** v1.0
**最后更新：** 2026-03-28

## 当前修饰器

```
COMPOSITION MODIFIER FOR UPPER GARMENT:
- The upper body garment is the hero product — it must occupy 60%+ of the frame
- Frame from mid-thigh or above, ensuring the entire garment is visible
- The model's face should be clearly visible and well-lit
- Lower body should wear simple, neutral-colored pants/skirt that does NOT compete with the hero garment
- Camera angle: straight-on or very slight downward angle to emphasize the upper body
```
