# 服装类型修饰器 — 下装

**版本：** v1.0
**最后更新：** 2026-03-28

## 当前修饰器

```
COMPOSITION MODIFIER FOR LOWER GARMENT:
- The lower body garment is the hero product — it must occupy 60%+ of the frame
- Frame must include the full length of the garment, from waistband to hem/shoes
- The model's face can be partially visible or cropped — the focus is NOT on the face
- Upper body should wear a simple, tucked-in, neutral-colored top that does NOT compete with the hero garment
- Camera angle: straight-on or very slight upward angle to emphasize the lower body and leg line
```
